About This Project

The Timer & To-Do List project was developed to help users enhance their productivity by providing a simple and efficient way to manage their tasks. The primary purpose of this project is to create a web-based application that allows users to keep track of their notes and reminders, ensuring that important tasks are not overlooked. The project addresses common needs for organization and time management by offering three distinct sections. Users can take regular notes, set timed reminders that notify them when the time is up, and even recover previously deleted notes. By combining these features, the application aims to provide a comprehensive tool for personal and professional task management. My goal is to create an intuitive and user-friendly interface that can be easily used by individuals from various backgrounds, helping them stay organized and manage their time more effectively.
